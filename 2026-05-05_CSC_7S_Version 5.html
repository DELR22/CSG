Synthesis · Write Your Value Proposition

You've researched your users, defined their shared needs, generated and selected your best ideas, and specified how they work together. Now distil everything into one sentence — the single promise this design makes.

Try: "A space that [your design move] — so that [your primary user] can [their core aspiration]."

"A workplace where analog wisdom, mindful presence, and post-digital fluency share the same floor — and make each other better."

"A home that bends the boundary between inside and outside, so a family of introverts can breathe."

"A retreat that holds the tension between solitude and connection — giving each person exactly as much of both as they need."
